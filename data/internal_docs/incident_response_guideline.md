# Incident Response Guideline

## Severity Levels
- Sev1: Service outage or data breach suspected
- Sev2: Major feature unavailable
- Sev3: Minor degradation

## Initial Response
- Acknowledge within 15 minutes for Sev1.
- Start an incident channel and assign an incident commander.

## Communication
- Provide updates every 30 minutes for Sev1.
- Do not share unverified root cause externally.
